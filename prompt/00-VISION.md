# Vision

## Project

Nikhil Nishad Portfolio v2

---

# Purpose

Build a portfolio that feels like a premium AI product instead of a traditional developer portfolio.

The website should communicate engineering quality, product thinking, and attention to detail while showcasing AI, Full Stack, and modern web development expertise.

The portfolio itself should act as the strongest project.

---

# Brand Positioning

Not

> Frontend Developer

Not

> MERN Stack Developer

Instead

> AI Engineer
> Full Stack Developer
> Building intelligent products and modern SaaS experiences.

---

# Goals

- Create a memorable first impression within 10 seconds.
- Showcase engineering quality through design and implementation.
- Highlight real-world AI and product experience.
- Increase interview callbacks.
- Demonstrate frontend craftsmanship.
- Present projects as products, not assignments.
- Build a scalable portfolio that evolves over time.
